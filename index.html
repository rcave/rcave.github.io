About US

Nautical Gnar Track Team is a non-profit, non-business cycling team working with the American Diabetes Association and Mad Opportunities. Our main goal is to promote fun, inclusion, and good sportsmanship. We have/will all signed sportsmanship contracts which state that we will do our best to help others succeed, respect other teams and riders, try our hardest to represent ourselves and sponsors well, and promote the love of cycling. We will be donating race winnings to ADA and Mad Opportunities and will be accepting donations for both.
